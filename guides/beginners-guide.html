📚 Japanese Pokemon Cards: Beginner Guide

🎴 Why Japanese Cards?

  • Better quality — Sharper printing, better centering
  • Earlier releases — Sets drop 3-6 months before English
  • Cheaper packs — ¥180 (~$1.20) vs $4.50 English
  • Exclusive promos — Many never released in English

🔍 How to Spot Fakes

  • Light test — Real cards block light, fakes glow through
  • Texture — Holos have distinct texture patterns
  • Font — Fakes often have wrong fonts or spacing
  • Blue core — Tear test (last resort): real cards have blue layer
💡 If a deal seems too good, it probably is. Vintage holos under ¥500 at flea markets warrant extra scrutiny.
