💰 Flea Market Price Guide — What to Pay

🎯 Reality check: Flea market sellers often know pack values. "Steal" prices (under ¥500 for premium packs) are rare — maybe 1 in 20 booths. "Acceptable" prices are realistic targets. "Walk away" = you're paying card shop prices with no guarantee.

⚡ Flea Market Reality

What you'll actually find:

  • Most sellers price at 70-90% of card shop prices — still profitable but not "steals"
  • Grandma selling grandson's old cards = best finds (doesn't know current prices)
  • Bulk lots (10+ random packs) often have hidden gems priced as commons
  • Negotiate! Start at 50% of asking, settle at 70%. "まとめて買うと安くなりますか？" works
  • Sunday afternoon = sellers want to go home, more willing to deal
