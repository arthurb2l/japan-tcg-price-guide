🗣️ Useful Phrases

Show these to vendors or speak the romaji pronunciation

📱 Screenshot this page for offline use!

👋 Greeting

こんにちは
Konnichiwa
Hello

🔍 Asking for Cards

ポケモンカードありますか？
Pokemon kaado arimasu ka?
Do you have Pokemon cards?
ワンピースカードありますか？
Wan Piisu kaado arimasu ka?
Do you have One Piece cards?

💴 Price Inquiry

これいくらですか？
Kore ikura desu ka?
How much is this?
全部でいくらですか？
Zenbu de ikura desu ka?
How much for all of these?

🤝 Negotiating

もう少し安くなりますか？
Mou sukoshi yasuku narimasu ka?
Can you go a little cheaper?
〇〇円でいいですか？
[Number] en de ii desu ka?
Would ¥[X] be okay?
💡 Tip: Replace 〇〇 with your price. Example: 千円 (sen en) = ¥1,000, 五百円 (go-hyaku en) = ¥500

✅ Buying

これください
Kore kudasai
I'll take this
全部ください
Zenbu kudasai
I'll take all of these

❌ Declining

考えます
Kangaemasu
I'll think about it
大丈夫です
Daijoubu desu
No thank you / I'm okay

🙏 Thanks

ありがとうございます
Arigatou gozaimasu
Thank you very much

🔢 Numbers (for prices)

百円 / 五百円 / 千円
Hyaku en / Go-hyaku en / Sen en
¥100 / ¥500 / ¥1,000
二千円 / 三千円 / 五千円
Ni-sen en / San-zen en / Go-sen en
¥2,000 / ¥3,000 / ¥5,000
一万円
Ichi-man en
¥10,000
💡 Pro tip: At flea markets, showing your phone calculator with a number works great for negotiating!
