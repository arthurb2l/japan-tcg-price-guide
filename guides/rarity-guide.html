💎 Rarity Guide

Understanding card rarities helps you identify valuable pulls and build your collection.

Pokemon TCG

⚫

Common (C)

Most basic cards, found multiple per pack

◆

Uncommon (U)

Slightly rarer, 2-3 per pack typically

⭐

Rare (R)

One per pack, holofoil versions exist

⭐⭐

Double Rare (RR)

Pokemon ex cards, ~1 in 3 packs

🌟

Ultra Rare (UR)

Full art cards, ~1 in 10 packs

🎨

Illustration Rare (IR)

Special artwork, highly collectible

✨

Special Art Rare (SAR)

Premium full art, chase cards

👑

Hyper Rare (HR)

Gold cards, rarest pulls
