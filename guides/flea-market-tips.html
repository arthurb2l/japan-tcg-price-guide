🏪 Pokemon Card Hunting in Japan

🗺️ Where to Hunt

Flea Markets フリーマーケット

  • Ohi Keibajo (Tokyo) — Largest, weekends only. Arrive by 7am.
  • Yoyogi Park (Tokyo) — Sundays. Tourist-heavy but occasional finds.
  • Shitennoji (Osaka) — 21st/22nd monthly. Less picked-over than Tokyo.

💡 Pro tip: Hard-Off "junk" bins (ジャンク) are goldmines. Staff often don't know card values.

💴 What to Pay

Flea Market Expectations

  • Bulk commons: ¥10-50 each, or ¥500 for a stack
  • Holos/rares: ¥100-500 (often underpriced)
  • Vintage (Base-Neo): ¥500-2000 (huge variance)
  • Sealed packs: Rare find, expect 50-70% market
⚠️ Red flags: If prices seem too good, check for fakes. Vintage reprints exist.
