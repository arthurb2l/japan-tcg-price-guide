📋 Card Condition Guide

Understanding card conditions helps you accurately value your collection and make fair trades.

NM Near Mint

  • Card looks fresh from pack
  • No visible wear, scratches, or whitening
  • Corners are sharp and clean
  • Surface is pristine with no marks
💰 Price Impact: 100% of market value

LP Lightly Played

  • Minor edge wear or light scratches
  • Slight corner whitening
  • May have been sleeved but played
  • Still presentable in a binder
💰 Price Impact: ~80-90% of NM value

MP Moderately Played

  • Noticeable wear on edges and corners
  • Surface scratches visible
  • May have minor creases
  • Card is still intact and readable
💰 Price Impact: ~60-70% of NM value

HP Heavily Played

  • Significant wear throughout
  • Major creases or bends
  • Heavy whitening on edges
  • Still tournament legal if sleeved
💰 Price Impact: ~40-50% of NM value
